NodeSchool in SMU

We are excited to introduce NodeSchool Chapters right here in SMU. It is a workshop featuring an interactive peer-learning format guided by mentors, have fun and feel at ease while learning JavaScript and NodeJS with your friends! We will have something for learners at all levels.

JavaScript is the language of today’s web. According to RedMonk, an industry analyst company, JavaScript ranks as the first technology used and talked worldwide. It become an “everywhere” language with the introduction of NodeJS that … you know what - if you’ve read till here, why don’t you just come on down and join us!

We are dedicated and driven to helping you level up from wherever you may be on your journey of learning. Take your first steps together with us to discover something new and pick up a useful skill!
